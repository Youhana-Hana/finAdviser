Welcome to FinAdviser










We will soon launch our powerful artificial intelligence tool Adorra. She will do all the financial thinking and planning for you so that you can spend more time with those that matter most.  

How it works

We use historical financial data to assess your financial record in order to help you create an actionable plan.

Step 1: You tell us what you are planning to do (e.g. planning to buy a house, planning to have children, planning a holiday, etc.).

Step 2: Our artificial intelligence Financial Adviser tool Adorra will recommend the budget you can actually afford for the event and she will create for you a monthly repayment plan, having taken into account all of your existing reoccurring monthly financial commitments.

Other benefits

  • If you need it, Adorra can also help you nurse your finances back to health by helping to create a personal finance plan (e.g. repayment plan for personal loans and credits cards, etc.).
  • Source for you suitable loan recommendations from independent financial advisers.
  • Seek out the best products that matches your needs and preferences (e.g. utility suppliers, insurance, savings and pension, etc.).
